Questions for Engagement

  1. The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?
    • It specifies the URL of the page that will process the form data.
  2. The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?
    • It specifies the HTTP method to use when sending form data.
  3. What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?
    • POST hides the form data from the URL, while GET appends the form data to the URL.
