Let's learn the basic Verb, Noun, and Adjective.

Verb

A verb is a word that describes an action or a state of being. For example, "run", "jump", "eat", "sleep" are all verbs.

Noun

A noun is a word that names a person, place, thing, or idea. For example, "dog", "house", "apple", "love" are all nouns.

Adjective

An adjective is a word that describes or gives more information about a noun. For example, "big", "red", "happy", "tasty" are all adjectives.
