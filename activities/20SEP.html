Definitions for the Engagement Assignment

Absolute Link - It is used for other websites, or your own website.
Relative Link - It is used for your website only.
List the 3 rules with relative links:
1. one dot (.) - current folder (not used)
2. two dots (..) - parent folder
3. slash (/) at beginning (start at the root)
