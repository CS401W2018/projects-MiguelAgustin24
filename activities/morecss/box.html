Block Tags

  • What is the difference between block tags and inline tags. Block-level tags create larger structures on the page. They typically start on a new line. While, inline tags do not start on a new line and only take up as much width as necessary.
  • What is the margin, border, and padding? Margin is the space outside the border, padding is the space inside the border, and border is the line that separates the padding from the margin.
  • What are units of measurement you can use in CSS? px, em, rem, %, vh, vw, cm, mm, in, pt, pc
  • When would you use a figure tag? When you want to add a photo or image to your page.
